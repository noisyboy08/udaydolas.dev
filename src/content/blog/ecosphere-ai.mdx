## Overview

EcosphereAI is a comprehensive AI-powered climate risk management platform designed to help organizations monitor, analyze, and respond to climate-related risks. The platform provides real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and sustainability insights.

![EcosphereAI dashboard showing climate risk analytics](/images/ecosphere.png)

## Key Features

- **🌍 Climate Risk Assessment** - Comprehensive risk analysis and evaluation
- **📊 Predictive Analytics** - Advanced forecasting for climate trends
- **🔄 Real-time Monitoring** - Continuous tracking of environmental metrics
- **🌱 Sustainability Insights** - Detailed sustainability reporting and recommendations
- **📈 Comprehensive Reporting** - Detailed analytics and visualization

## Tech Stack

- **AI/ML**: Machine learning models for climate prediction
- **Data Analytics**: Advanced analytics engine
- **Real-time Processing**: Live data monitoring systems
